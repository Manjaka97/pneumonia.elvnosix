Deep Learning for Pneumonia Detection

Because of the incredible progress we have seen in the last few years, we can now use Machine Learning to help solve problems in various fields. This simple web application aims to do that in the field of medicine. It is an application that detects signs of Pneumonia in a person. It is very easy to use. A chest X-ray scan of the patient just needs to be uploaded, and within a few seconds, the application will determine if signs of Pneumonia have been detected from the scan.

When to use?

From lung.org, here are the symptoms of Pneumonia:

  • Cough, which may produce greenish, yellow or even bloody mucus
  • Fever, sweating and shaking chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Rapid, shallow breathing
  • Sharp or stabbing chest pain that gets worse when you breathe deeply or cough
  • Loss of appetite, low energy, and fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting, especially in small children
  • Confusion, especially in older people
